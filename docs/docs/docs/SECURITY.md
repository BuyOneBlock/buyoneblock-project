# Security policy

## 1. Scope

This security policy applies to:

- the Buy One Block smart contracts,
- the 1BONEBU utility token,
- the Buy One Block documentation and related repositories.

It does **not** cover third‑party platforms, wallets, exchanges, or any external integrations.

---

## 2. No financial guarantees

Buy One Block and the 1BONEBU token:

- do **not** guarantee profits or returns,
- do **not** represent ownership of any asset,
- do **not** grant rights to company revenues or governance.

The token is a **utility & access token**, as documented in the whitepapers and token‑utility documentation.

---

## 3. Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability related to:

- the smart contract,
- the way the token is used,
- or the documentation that could lead to misuse,

please **do not disclose it publicly**.

Instead, contact the team directly at:

- **Email:** security@buyoneblock.com *(example, à adapter si besoin)*

When reporting, please include:

- a clear description of the issue,
- steps to reproduce (if applicable),
- any relevant transaction hashes, addresses, or links.

---

## 4. Responsible disclosure

We ask all security researchers to:

- act in **good faith**,
- avoid compromising user funds,
- avoid privacy violations,
- not perform attacks that could disrupt the ecosystem.

We commit to:

- acknowledge valid reports,
- assess and prioritize fixes,
- improve our documentation and contracts when needed.

---

## 5. Smart contract status

- The contract is deployed on **BNB Smart Chain**.
- Contract address: `0x6cc08f61612c14098f9b230a474ee34d9584d56e`
- The contract source is verified on BscScan.
- Any update or migration will be documented in the repository and on official channels.

---

## 6. Out of scope

The following are **out of scope** for vulnerability reports:

- loss of private keys, seed phrases, or wallet access,
- phishing attacks outside of official Buy One Block channels,
- issues with third‑party platforms (exchanges, wallets, bridges),
- speculative price movements or market volatility.

---

## 7. Legal notice

By submitting a security report, you agree to comply with:

- applicable laws in your jurisdiction,
- ethical security research principles.

Buy One Block does not authorize any activity that would:

- intentionally damage the ecosystem,
- exploit vulnerabilities for personal gain,
- violate user privacy or applicable regulations.
